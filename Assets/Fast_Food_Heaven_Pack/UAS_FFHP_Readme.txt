FAST FOOD HEAVEN PACK 4.0

This delicious asset pack contains 94 fast food items and prefab meal combinations made with love and care!

The models are between 6 and 421 polygons. The package comes with 1024x1024 PBR textures (albedo, normal, metallic/smoothness). All the models are available as FBX files. The asset pack is compatible with built-in & URP. Lightweight enough for mobile projects, detailed enough for VR!

This package is updated every now and then and it's price will go up, but everyone who already owns the package will of course get all updates for free.
